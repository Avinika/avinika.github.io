• Ukraine-Russia War - Russian Equipment Losses Visualization

    Description of the project

    This project describes about the losses of Russian equipment during the war with Ukraine from February 25th to July 25th, 2022. Russian invasion of Ukraine started from February 2022 and still continuous. Russia has been using several types of troops to invade Ukraine like land force, Air force and navy. The loss of equipment of these troops has been visualized in each slide along with the death toll of Russian personnel.

    The purpose of this visualization is to explore the following questions.

    1. Which day has the highest loss of air force equipment

    2. In which period Russia has lost land force military equipment in Ukraine and why?
